Holiday Reading List

Twas the day after classes, when all through the school
Not a student was stirring, as a general rule.
The textbooks were left by the chimney to burn,
In hopes that those lessons would never return.

The students were nestled all snug in their beds,
While visions of coding swirled in their heads.
The profs at their desks, in full grading-mode,
With coffee in hand, for marking overload.

When out in the hall arose such a clatter,
I sprang from my desk to see what was the matter.
Away to the door I flew like a flash,
To see one last student fly by in a dash!

They wanted more books, texts, and articles to read.
Lucky for them, I could satisfy that need.
I cobbled together a list with such speed,
And left that list with with the student to read:

  • Style vs Design by Jeffrey Zeldman
  • The Colors of Grief by Alex Charchar
  • From Metaphor to Maturity by Erik Spiekermann
  • Text by Ellen Lupton
  • The Design Lesson by Andy Rutledge
  • Delight and Accommodation by Frank Chimero
  • What screens Want by Frank Chimero
  • Ear Trumpets and Bionic Superpowers by Karen McGrane
  • All our Yesterdays by Jeremy Keith
  • As We May Link by Jeremy Keith
  • Get Started With GitHub Pages (Plus Bonus Jekyll) by Anna Debenham
  • A Dao of Web Design by John Allsopp
  • On Web Typography by Jason Santa Maria
  • How We Read by Jason Santa Maria
